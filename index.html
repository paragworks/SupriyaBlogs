Vizag Trip

A week ago, I embarked on a 07-day adventure in Vizag. My journey began with a visit to Kailash Giri, where I marveled at the impressive Shiv Shakti monument. Next, I hopped on a charming toy train, enjoying breathtaking aerial views. Then, I reached the most scenic spot on Kailash Giri, offering an almost panoramic view of Vizag, a sight I'd always yearned for.

 

Afterward, I treated myself to some delicious Pani puri. My exploration continued as I headed to Rushikonda Beach, renowned for its Blue Flag status. I also had the unique opportunity to explore the interior of a sample submarine and explore the fascinating aircraft museum. It was an unforgettable 7-day journey filled with incredible experiences!

 

The next morning, my adventure continued as I visited Thotlakonda Beach. It held a remarkable natural arch-like formation, unlike anything I'd ever witnessed before. In the evening, I had the privilege of gazing upon the awe-inspiring Dolphin Nose Lighthouse, offering breathtaking vistas. To cap off my day, I made my way to Yarada Beach, a tranquil haven almost hidden by rugged rocks. The serenity there, far from the bustling city, was truly peaceful and refreshing.

 

After that day, I had to attend my Bua's house inauguration and ended up staying there for 4 days. It was such a heartwarming reunion with my family after 5 long years. In return, my Bua and her family showered me with generous gifts of money and clothing (In India, It is the ingrained culture of giving. Our culture emphasizes sharing with our guests. We treat them as representatives of God (athiti devo bhava) and send them with sweets and gifts to remember the happy time spent together)
